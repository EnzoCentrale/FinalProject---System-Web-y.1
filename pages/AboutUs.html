About Us

We are a group of eco-conscious students passionate about sustainability, outdoor gear, and giving quality clothing a second life. Our platform connects people who love brands like Salomon, Arc'teryx, Patagonia, and more — but want to shop in a more responsible way.

Our Mission

Our goal is simple: reduce waste by extending the life of high-quality outdoor and streetwear clothing. Instead of letting items end up in landfills, we help them find new owners who appreciate performance, durability, and style.

What We Offer

  • Curated second-hand products from trusted eco-friendly brands
  • Affordable prices compared to new retail items
  • Surveys that let you give your opinion and earn discounts
  • A clean, simple, and modern shopping experience

Why Second-Hand?

Fashion is one of the most polluting industries in the world. Choosing second-hand reduces carbon emissions, saves water, and cuts waste—while still giving you access to high-end performance gear.

Our Values

  • Sustainability — choosing better for the planet
  • Transparency — clear product descriptions and conditions
  • Community — user feedback and surveys help shape our catalog

Meet the Team

We’re a small team of students who love nature, hiking, trail running, and ethical fashion. This project is part of our HTML course, but also a real commitment to promoting sustainable choices.
