QandA

Where do the products you sell come from?

Our items come from individuals, partner organizations, and brands committed to sustainable practices.

Are the products authentic?

Yes. Every item is checked by our team before being listed on the website.

What brands can I find on your platform?

You can find eco-friendly and durable brands such as Muji, Patagonia, Arc’teryx, and more.

How does your pricing work?

Prices depend on the condition, brand, rarity, and second-hand market value.

How does the discount survey system work?

You can take short surveys to earn discounts or reward points.

Can I return an item?

You have 14 days after delivery to request a return.

How can I track my order?

You will receive a tracking link once your order is shipped.

How do you ensure ecological responsibility?

We prioritize second-hand items, reused packaging, and eco-friendly logistics.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and gift cards.

How can I contact support?

You can contact us through the support form or via email.
